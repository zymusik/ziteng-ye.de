Ye Ziteng,from China，born in 1992. He got Composition bachelor in Wuhan Conservatory of Music. He won the 3rd Prize for the 2014 China-Asian Music Week. In 2015 he won the 3rd Prize 2015 SAMADIS’ International Composition Competition. His flute work Self-portrait-I record by Orlando Cela, on the “Project Extended” 2016 .
